What is BESI-C?

Behavioral and Environmental Sensing and Intervention for Cancer (BESI-C) is a Smart Health sensing system designed to monitor, predict and manage cancer pain in the home setting. BESI-C has been created by a team of engineers and clinicians at the University of Virginia to support patients, family caregivers and healthcare providers. Managing cancer pain at home can be stressful and difficult; BESI-C offers a user-friendly and non-invasive solution.
